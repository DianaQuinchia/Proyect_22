Costco CFO's $1.1 Million Sale
Five Costco Wholesale insiders, including the chief financial officer, made bulk sales of 64,342 shares for $7,541,151, an average of $117.20 each, from Oct. 16 through 31, as shares continue to trade near an all-time high.

Chief Financial Officer Richard A. Galanti sold 9,370 Costco (ticker: COST) shares for $1,109,744. He now directly holds 55,413 shares, a stake of less than 1%, and an additional 7,000 shares through North Island Holdings. Galanti's most recent previous transaction was on April 25 when he exercised options and sold 20,000 shares for $2.2 million, an average of $108.30 each.
